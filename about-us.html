About the team behind GRX10

Let's Dive In

GRX10 is a team of expert marketers with experience in delivering multimillion-dollar success in both business-to-business and consumer markets globally. Founded by graduates from the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology , Indian Institute of Management and INSEAD staffed with professionals from competitive industries like semiconductors, solar energy, fast-moving consumer goods, and e-commerce, we aim to provide top-notch marketing knowledge. Our goal is to help startups and large companies achieve rapid growth through effective product marketing, digital strategies, and e-commerce.

GRX 10 : Redefining Business Solutions

In an unforgiving business environment, akin to assembling an Airbus A320 with its 340,000 parts, we prioritize perfection. Our approach focuses on:
  • Quality Over Quantity

    Ensuring every solution we provide is of the highest caliber.

  • Innovative Success Metrics

    Measuring success through "Big-Hairy Ideas" that push the boundaries of conventional thinking.

  • Decentralized Decision-Making

Unlike traditional consulting firms or digital marketing agencies, we've structured GRX10 to directly address and solve our clients' problems. Our team is divided into four layers of competencies

  • Founders

  • Advisor's Network

    We tackle "Dark Areas" of general expertise by bringing in external advisors for specific, root cause analysis.

  • Planners

    Our planning experts translate high-level actions into manageable components, identifying resources and charting the path to swift execution.

  • Execution Team

    Comprising generalists adept at rapid task execution, monitoring progress through testing and overall funnel metrics.
